Stock Market

The stock market is a dynamic system where investors can buy, sell, and trade shares of publicly traded companies. It operates through exchanges, such as the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) and the Nasdaq, where stocks are listed and traded. At its core, the stock market functions as a marketplace where companies raise capital by offering ownership stakes in the form of shares, and investors can buy these shares to own a portion of the company.
